About the Game

Welcome to Teacher Chase - an exciting game where you take on the role of a teacher trying to maintain order in a chaotic classroom!

In this unique adventure, students wander around the classroom, and it's your job as the teacher to catch them and restore order. Use your special abilities to manage the classroom dynamics!

Key game features include:

  • Student mood system (happy and mad bars)
  • Special teacher abilities
  • Dynamic classroom environment
  • Strategic gameplay
